About HypeGirl

HypeGirl is an AI companion for everyday encouragement, homework help, age-appropriate facts, and supportive chat. HypeGirl includes a parent dashboard for reviewing sensitive or urgent messages.

Not Professional Advice

HypeGirl is not a therapist, doctor, lawyer, emergency responder, teacher, or substitute for a trusted adult. HypeGirl may be wrong, incomplete, or inappropriate for a specific situation.

Emergencies

Do not use HypeGirl for emergencies. If someone may be in immediate danger, call 911. For crisis support in the U.S., call or text 988.

Parent Responsibilities

Parents are responsible for creating and supervising family accounts, sharing family codes, reviewing parent queue messages, managing subscriptions, and deciding whether HypeGirl is appropriate for their child.

Child Use

Children should use HypeGirl with parent permission. Children should not share private information, passwords, addresses, payment information, or emergency situations with the app instead of a trusted adult.

Subscriptions

HypeGirl may offer a free message limit and a paid family plan for unlimited daily messages. Subscription billing and cancellation are handled through Stripe.

Changes

These terms may change as HypeGirl develops. Continued use of HypeGirl means the family accepts the current terms.
