Privacy

Hype Girl stores account details, chat messages, safety classifications, parent queue items, and optional avatar data in Firebase.

Messages classified as AMBER or RED may be shown to a linked parent or trusted adult for safety review.

Do not use this app as an emergency service. If someone may be in immediate danger, call 911. For crisis support in the U.S., call or text 988.

For production launch, replace this starter notice with a lawyer-reviewed privacy policy and child-safety data retention policy.
